About us

Greetings from Mbhaco Designs!


Our journey in online shopping started in 2020 when we saw that there was a lack of cultural online shopping stores in South Africa, We then decided to create one and that we want to show the world our heritage but overall we have been around since 2014.

What we do:

We create and design custom Xhosa attires for anyone who would likes Xhosa patterns or just patterns in general. We have a variety of patterns and colors to choose from and we also have accessories to go well with our designs. We also do custom designs for celebrations such as weddings, matric balls etc.
